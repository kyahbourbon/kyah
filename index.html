About me.

My name is Kyah and I am happy that you're here. I earned a Bachelors of Business Administration with a major in data analytics and statistics and a concentration in Information management at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. I have a passion for learning and understanding. Analytics is perfect for me because I am able to not only learn, but also interpret my understanding to others and bring more light to otherwise dim situations. I am a first-generation college student (woo!). I am the education trailblazer in my family and I am honored to change the lives of future generations; even though it wasn't always easy, it will be worth it. I hope to accomplish similar goals in my professional career as well. My long term goal is to work for the government sector doing analytical work. I am a innovator when necessary, but I pride myself on my integrity and drive to do good for any community. Goals change frequently but, the motivations behind them are slower to change.

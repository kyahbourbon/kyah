A sneak peak into...

who I am.

Born in Hagerstown, Maryland to a small family of East Coast natives. I grew up in East Tennessee and enjoyed many snow days due to the insane curvature of country roads. Being born in the year 2000 and a member of "Gen Z" has definitely been an experience. I used to envy the people who were of age during the early 2000's because everything was new and exciting. People who were born after 2008 will never know life before ground breaking technology, they will just expect it to work properly. But I do see my generation making quakes in the way we use technology and how we think about it. I am a part of the generation who is not afraid of change.

So what?

I am in the new generation of analysts that grew up alongside our modern technology. While I expect technology to work, I also want to know how it works. I hope to differentiate myself by bringing my creative perspective to try and solve old and new problems. Business/Data anaytlics primarily caught my attention because it is very calculated. Making data driven decisions boosts confidence and brings security. I think most companies could use an extra layer of security when making big, or small, decisions.
